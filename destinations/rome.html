Popular Destinations in Rome

Colosseum

Explore the ancient Roman amphitheater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Vatican City

Visit the smallest independent state in the world and explore St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and Vatican Museums.

Trevi Fountain

Make a wish and toss a coin into the famous baroque fountain, one of the most iconic landmarks in Rome.
