Popular Destinations in Rio de Janeiro

Christ the Redeemer

Visit the iconic statue of Christ the Redeemer, one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, offering panoramic views of Rio de Janeiro.

Copacabana Beach

Relax on the famous Copacabana Beach, known for its white sand, vibrant atmosphere, and iconic boardwalk.

Sugarloaf Mountain

Ride the cable car to the top of Sugarloaf Mountain and enjoy breathtaking views of Rio de Janeiro and Guanabara Bay.
